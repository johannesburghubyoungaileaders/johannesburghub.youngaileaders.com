About Us

Creating a community of young AI leaders in Johannesburg

Johannesburg Hub ‚Äì Young AI Leaders (YAIL) is a vibrant and dynamic community dedicated to nurturing the next generation of AI talent in South Africa and beyond. As the Johannesburg Hub Leader for the Young AI Leaders community, we are building a network of forward-thinkers aged 18-30 who are passionate about using AI to create positive change aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Founded in 2025, our mission is to bridge the AI skills gap in Africa by empowering young leaders to develop AI-driven solutions that tackle both local and global challenges. We achieve this by providing education, networking opportunities, and practical experience to young professionals, students, and AI enthusiasts.

Through the AIforGood platform sessions, and collaborative projects, we are laying the foundation for AI innovation in Johannesburg that will drive meaningful impact across the continent.

Our Commitment to Trustworthy and Equitable AI

Championing responsible AI development for a better future

At Johannesburg Hub Young AI Leaders, we believe that artificial intelligence must be developed and deployed with human wellbeing at its core. We are committed to fostering AI systems that are fair, transparent, accountable, and beneficial to all segments of society, especially those historically marginalized.

Ethical AI

We advocate for and implement ethical frameworks that guide AI development. This includes ensuring fairness, transparency, and accountability in AI systems, and mitigating harmful biases that can perpetuate social inequalities. Our members are trained to recognize ethical considerations in every stage of the AI lifecycle.

Global AI Governance

We actively participate in discussions around AI governance, ensuring African perspectives are represented. We believe in developing governance frameworks that balance innovation with safeguards to protect human rights and dignity, while reflecting the diverse needs and contexts of the Global South.

Innovation for Sustainable Development

We focus on leveraging AI to address Africa's sustainable development challenges. Our projects align with the UN Sustainable Development Goals, using AI to tackle issues such as healthcare access, climate change adaptation, education quality, and resource management across the continent.

AI for the Good Society

We promote AI as a force for social good. Our community emphasizes applications that enhance human capabilities, foster inclusive economic growth, and strengthen democratic processes. We believe technology should serve people and communities, not the other way around.

Join Our Community

Whether you're an AI expert, a curious beginner, or an organization looking to leverage ethical AI, there's a place for you in our community.
